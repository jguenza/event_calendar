Seven-Dish Christmas Dinner

24 December 2025 • Timezone: Europe/Amsterdam • Seven courses, one evening
A Belgian and a Northeastern Brazilian walk into a seven-course dinner with two Dutch sisters—what could possibly go wrong?
This is exactly how international diplomacy works—one plate at a time.

Dish 1 • Welcome

Opening•Panettone met Koffie & Thee alla Gezelligheid
Pistachio panettone from Sicily meets Christmas bread from the Roman bakery in Elst—served with coffee, tea, and zero respect for borders.

Dish 2 • Sweet Relief

After Walk•Fraises au Chocolat de Belgique
Belgian strawberries in Dutch chocolate, paired with demi-sec Champagne de Castellane.

Dish 3 • Game & Spain

Table Game•Pequeño Recuerdo de España
Olives with anchovies, crisp chips, Take 5 on the table, non-alcoholic spritz and cava.

Dish 4 • Sea

Main Sea•Décadence Nordestine à la Thermidor
Lobster Thermidor, asparagus, king crab tapenade. Paired with Anjou Blanc “La Grande Croix”.

Dish 5 • Passage

Transition•Brie à la Truffe
The crossing from sea to land—border officially removed.

Dish 6 • Land

Main Land•Wild Stufato alla Toscana
Wild boar and deer pasta, finished without mercy. Paired with Syrah “À Deux Pas” (2018).

Dish 7 • Finale

Dessert•Tiramisù Classico
Classic Italian ending with English tea.
